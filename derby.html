Scoring Rules

Random Draw: All 19 horses are dealt round-robin to players. Owner of the 1st-place horse takes the entire random-draw pot.

Strategic Picks: Each player picks a WIN horse + 2 SHOW horses. 5 pts if your WIN pick finishes 1st. +2 pts for every one of your 3 picks that finishes top 3 (max 6 pts).

Ties: Pot splits evenly among tied winners.

No strategic winner (every score zero): pot rolls into the random-draw pot.
